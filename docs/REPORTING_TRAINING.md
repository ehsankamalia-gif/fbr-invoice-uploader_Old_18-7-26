# Reporting Portal Training (End Users)

## 1) Open the Reporting Portal
- Open the application.
- Go to the Reports section.
- Click Open Dashboard.

The portal opens in your browser at `http://localhost:9000/dashboard`.

## 2) Use Filters (Date + Status)
On the dashboard:
- Select From and To dates.
- Select Status:
  - All
  - Pending
  - Success
  - Failed
- Click Apply.

## 3) Auto Refresh
- Click Auto to toggle live refresh.
- When Auto is enabled, the dashboard refreshes every 15 seconds.

## 4) Exports
Use the export buttons:
- CSV: quick spreadsheet export
- Excel: structured export for finance teams
- PDF: printable summary report
- PowerPoint: slide deck for management review

Exports use the current template and filters.

## 5) Templates (Drag & Drop)
Open Template Builder.
- Select a template from the dropdown.
- Drag widgets from the Available Widgets list into the Layout area.
- Use Remove to delete a widget from the layout.
- Click Save to apply changes.

Common widgets:
- Total Invoices
- Total Amount
- Avg Invoice
- Daily Sales chart
- Status Breakdown chart
- Invoices table

## 6) Scheduled Reports (Automatic)
Open Schedules.
- Choose a template.
- Set Interval (minutes).
- Choose Format.
- Enter Recipients (comma-separated emails).
- Click Create Schedule.

If email is configured by IT, scheduled reports will be emailed automatically.

## 7) Best Practices
- Use Daily Sales + Status Breakdown for operational monitoring.
- Use PDF for compliance/printouts.
- Use PowerPoint for monthly performance reviews.
- Keep invoice table widgets limited to key fields for performance.
